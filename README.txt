Craftify for Minecraft
========================

Craftify is a sleek iOS app designed for Minecraft players to browse, search,
and manage crafting recipes with ease.

CREDIT
------
This app was originally created by Dave Van Cauwenberghe for iOS / Apple devices.
I did NOT make the original app. Dave did.

I only copied the design and ported it to Windows (WPF / .NET).
